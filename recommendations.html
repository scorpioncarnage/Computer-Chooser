Recommendations

Providing a catalogue of recomended computers for your specific needs

Best Value

Acer Swift 3

Best Value

With an emphasis on minimalism and an incredible focus on what really matters in a laptop, the Acer Swift series is prominant in great value laptops. Expect great performance and an impressive experience for its surprising price!

Best Performance

Razer Blade 15

Best Performance

The best performance laptops are usually gaming laptops, but the Razer Blade 15 stands out among its peers in being a stealthy and thin laptop that makes minimal compromises in performance.

Best Longevity

Apple Macbook Air 2020

Best Longevity

One of the best and well-rounded macbooks, the 2020 model of this beloved laptop features a powerful Apple Silicon M1 processor for lightning fast speed and longevity. With incredible long-time support, expect this device to last for 7+ years.

Best Windows

Dell XPS 13 9310

Best Windows

Dell has a reputation for unbelievably fast and well performing laptops. The XPS 13 is one of their most beloved series and stands out from the competition in its well rounded design that encapsulates its attention to detail and focus on creating the best windows laptop for all users.

Best Mac

Apple Macbook Pro 2021

Best Mac

The Apple Silicon M1 processor in 2020 revolutionized the performance of laptops, but in 2021 Apple released the Macbook Pro with the M1 Pro processor. In addition to the impressive Macbook Pro, this new iteration definitely takes the cake as the best Macbook.

Best Chromebook

Acer Spin 311

Best Chromebook

Acer is a very reputable manufacturer, and their Spin 311 Chromebook is no exception to their impecable quality in laptops. The ChromeOS is incredibly intuitve and perfect for students and first-time computer owners.

Best Value

Apple Mac Mini 2020

Best Value

With its unbelievably small footprint, this tiny machine packs an incredible punch as a great value desktop computer. An intuitive and easy computer for all users, the 2020 Mac Mini has amazing versatility and ease of use, but with an appealing pricetag that will surprise you for the incredible performance this computer provides!

Best Performance

iBUYPOWER Element MR 208i

Best Performance

When it comes to performance in desktops, the sky is the limit, but also is the price tag! High performance desktops can easily reach astronomical prices, while most users would be wasting money on unused potential. But for less than 2 grand, the iBUYPOWER Element MR 208i provides incredible performance and even includes a modern chasis with RGB accent lights!

Best Longevity

Dell Inspiron 7700

Best Longevity

Dell is a fantastic manufacturer of high-quality computers that last for longer than most of the competition. Specifically with the Inspiron 7700, the processor will continue to give high end performance for years and the SSD provides lightning fast speeds and durability as well.

Best Windows

Microsoft Surface Studio 2

Best Windows

Microsoft's Surface series is a well-known and liked collection of impressive computers with unique designs and features. The Surface Studio 2 is a stunning desktop that is also a versatile media creation machine.

Best Mac

Apple iMac 2021

Best Mac

Boasting one of the smallest form factors in an all-in-one computer, the 2021 iMac with an Apple Silicon M1 chip offers fantastic performance for a compelling price point. Additionally, this iMac comes in multiple unique color options to choose from!

Best Chromebook

HP Chromebase

Best Chromebook

With one of the most unique and well-designed form factors in all-in-one's on the market, the HP Chromebase offers an excellent computing experience for a tempting price point. It's large display and super intuitive operating system makes this computer a wonderful choice for any user.
